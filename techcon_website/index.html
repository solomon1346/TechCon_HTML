History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small tech meetup and has grown into a global conference attracting thousands of technology enthusiasts each year.

Mission

Our mission is to connect innovators, developers, and entrepreneurs to shape the future of technology through collaboration, workshops, and keynote presentations.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted renowned speakers such as Ada Lovelace, Alan Turing, and Grace Hopper, who have inspired attendees with their contributions to computing and technology.
